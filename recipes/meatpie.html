Meat pie

Description

This is a french pie recipe

Beef, pork, and potato are all nicely spiced and baked together!

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • Vegetables
  • Spices and seasonings
  • 1/4 cup of water
  • Pie crust

Steps

  1. Boil and mash the potatoes.
  2. Cook the meat with the water, onion, garlic and seasonings.
  3. Mix in the potatoes, then fill the pie crust.
  4. Bake until the crust is browned
